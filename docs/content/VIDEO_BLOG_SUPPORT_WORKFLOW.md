# Video Supporting Blog Posts — Workflow
**Purpose:** Short videos that drive traffic to wasatchwise.com blog. Native LinkedIn, repost elsewhere.

## Format
- **Length:** 1–2 minutes
- **Structure:** Bullet points on screen + you walking through them
- **CTA:** "Read the full post at wasatchwise.com/blog/[slug]"
- **Platform:** LinkedIn first (native video), then Twitter/X if desired

## Script Template (1–2 min)

1. **Hook (10 sec):** "If you're a superintendent adopting AI tools, here are the 5 questions you must answer first."
2. **Bullets (60–90 sec):** One by one, name each point. Keep it tight.
3. **CTA (15 sec):** "I wrote the full framework — including red flags and green lights — at wasatchwise.com/blog/[slug]. Link in the comments."

---

## Posting Cadence
- One video per week max (no brakes = don't slow the text flywheel)
- Match video to the blog post you published that week
- Add link in first comment for clickability
