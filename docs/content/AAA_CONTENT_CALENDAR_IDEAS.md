# Adult AI Academy — Content Calendar Ideas

**Purpose:** A bajillion ideas for AAA blog posts, short-form clips, and practical AI content. Mix of fear/gap/anxiety hooks, everyday practical use, and "here's a trick" value-first content.

**Target:** Gen X adults, ~8th grade reading level, plain-English.

## Content Calendar Suggestion (12 Weeks)

**Weeks 1–4: Practical everyday + quiz CTAs**
- Week 1: Blog: Fridge/meal plan. Short-form: 15s fridge, 30s quiz.
- Week 2: Blog: Notebook LM. Short-form: 15s Notebook LM, 45s fridge demo.
- Week 3: Blog: Summarize emails. Short-form: 15s drunk intern, 30s quiz.
- Week 4: Blog: Will AI take my job? Short-form: 15s quiz, 45s one trick + quiz.

**Weeks 5–8: Tricks and tips + gap content**
- Week 5: Blog: One prompt structure. Short-form: 15s prompt, 30s crumple zones.
- Week 6: Blog: Hallucination vs misgrounding. Short-form: 30s distinction, 15s quiz.
- Week 7: Blog: Chat with your insurance. Short-form: 15s insurance, 30s Notebook LM.
- Week 8: Blog: AI anxiety is real. Short-form: 30s don't panic, 45s why I built this.

**Weeks 9–12: Tool spotlights + seasonal**
- Week 9: Blog: Notebook LM for learners. Short-form: 30s chat with docs.
- Week 10: Blog: Free vs paid AI. Short-form: 15s walled garden, 30s vetting.
- Week 11: Blog: 10-minute vetting checklist. Short-form: 45s vetting demo.
- Week 12: Blog: AI for the holidays (or next seasonal). Short-form: 15s seasonal, 30s quiz.
