## TEC Student Data Privacy Alliance (SDPA)

### Overview

TEC SDPA is a collaborative service that helps school districts acquire signed Data Privacy Agreements (DPAs) with software vendors that collect student PII. SDPC Member; uses SDPC Resource Registry and National DPA framework.

### Service Model

| Type | States | Pricing |
|------|--------|---------|
| **Direct** | MA, ME, NH, NE, RI, VT, VA | $1/student, max $4,999/year |
| **Partnership** | IL, MO, NY, OH, TN | Via partner org |

**Partnership details:**
- Illinois → Learning Technology Center (LTC)
- Missouri → MOREnet
- New York → NY State DOE + 12 RICs
- Ohio → Learn21
- Tennessee → TETA

### Value Propositions

- Pre-negotiated DPAs with thousands of vendors
- SDPC National DPA framework
- Legal/administrative support for vendor negotiations
- Registry access; DPA uploads to SDPC Resource Registry

### ABYA Synergy

| Aspect | TEC SDPA | ABYA |
|--------|----------|------|
| Purpose | DPA procurement service | Education/awareness campaign |
| Audience | District admins, tech directors | Parents, educators, admins, students |
| Model | Paid ($1/student) | Free |
| Role | Do the DPA work for you | Teach you how to do it yourself |

**Use case:** ABYA educates → TEC executes. State ecosystem pages link to TEC for applicable states so administrators know where to get implementation support.

## Future of Privacy Forum (FPF) / Student Privacy Compass

### Overview

- **FPF:** Privacy leadership think tank advancing principled data practices
- **Student Privacy Compass:** One-stop resource center for K-12 student data privacy (formerly FERPA\|Sherpa)

### Focus Areas

- Youth Privacy
- AI/ML, Health, Biometrics
- U.S. state legislation

### Key Resources (relevant to ABYA)

- **Vetting Generative AI Tools for Use in Schools** (Apr 2024)
- **School Administrator's Guide to Addressing Deepfakes** (Mar 2025)
- **State Guidance on Use of Generative AI in K-12 Education** (Apr 2025)
- **Understanding Student Monitoring** (infographic)
- State legislation analysis; COPPA modernization updates

### ABYA Synergy

| Aspect | FPF / Student Privacy Compass | ABYA |
|--------|-------------------------------|------|
| Focus | Research & policy analysis | Practical action, workflows |
| Content | Policy papers, infographics, legal analysis | State workflows, certification, tools |
| Depth | Conceptual (trends, legislation) | Operational (DPAs, roles, workflows) |

**Use case:** ABYA links to FPF for emerging issues (AI vetting, deepfakes, monitoring), policy context, and supplemental reading. FPF could reference ABYA for operational implementation examples (e.g., Utah guide).
