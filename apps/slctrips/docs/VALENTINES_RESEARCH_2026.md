## Detailed profiles: TikTok Top 5 only

### 1. Lava Hot Springs, ID

- **Type:** Hot Springs Town  
- **Drive:** 2 hours / 115 miles via I-15 N  
- **Why romantic:** Mineral-rich, odor-free hot pools; quirky main street; spontaneous couples' soak.  
- **Best season:** Year-round. Winter soaks with snow = magical.  
- **3 activities:** Evening soak in World Famous Hot Pools (multiple temps); float Portneuf River (summer); explore downtown (antiques, cafes).  
- **Lodging:** Riverside Inn, Home Hotel & Motel, vacation rentals, RV/camping.  
- **Hook:** "brunch ‚Üí mineral soak ‚Üí river float ‚Üí cozy inn"  
- **Insider angle for TripKit:** Address comment-debate (e.g. "Why we included it: odor-free mineral pools, 2 hours flat, year-round‚Äîplus the downtown is genuinely charming. If you want more secluded, see Mystic Hot Springs or Homestead Crater.").

### 2. Bonneville Salt Flats, UT

- **Type:** Natural Wonder  
- **Drive:** 1.5 hours / 110 miles via I-80 W  
- **Why romantic:** Surreal white expanse, legendary sunset photo ops, otherworldly emptiness for a contemplative moment.  
- **Best season:** Late summer‚Äìfall (July‚ÄìOct) when dry. Spring can be flooded (mirror effect‚Äîalso stunning).  
- **3 activities:** Sunset photo shoot on the flats; Wendover casino dinner/entertainment; stargazing on the flats after dark.  
- **Lodging:** Wendover casinos (Montego Bay, Rainbow) or SLC day trip.  
- **Hook:** "brunch ‚Üí white infinity ‚Üí sunset portraits ‚Üí casino dinner"

### 3. Mirror Lake (Uinta Mountains), UT

- **Type:** Scenic drive / alpine lakes (Bucket: Mountain Lakes, Fall Color, Cabin Vibes)  
- **Drive:** ~1.5 hrs / ~75 mi (Mirror Lake Highway)  
- **Why romantic:** High-alpine lakes, fall color, cabin vibes, quiet escape.  
- **Note:** Seasonal‚Äîhigh-elevation; check road status in winter.  
- **Use research:** Mirror Lake Highway in "Mountain Lakes / Fall Color / Cabin Vibes" bucket; combine with nearby lakes and trails for copy.

### 4. Goblin Valley State Park, UT

- **Type:** State Park / Dark Sky  
- **Drive:** 4 hours / 215 miles via I-70  
- **Why romantic:** Mars-like hoodoos (goblins), certified International Dark Sky Park, surreal photography, night sky magic.  
- **Best season:** Spring and fall ideal. Summer hot. Winter quiet and accessible.  
- **3 activities:** Valley of Goblins at golden hour (photo ops); telescope night sky program (ranger schedule); Little Wild Horse Canyon slot hike (nearby).  
- **Lodging:** Yurts in park (book early), camping, motels in Green River.  
- **Hook:** "brunch ‚Üí Mars on Earth ‚Üí yurt under the Milky Way"

### 5. Great Basin National Park, NV

- **Type:** National Park / Dark Sky  
- **Drive:** 4 hours / 234 miles via US-50/US-6  
- **Why romantic:** Ancient bristlecone pines, Lehman Caves, one of the darkest skies in the continental U.S.‚Äîsolitude for two.  
- **Best season:** Late spring‚Äìfall (May‚ÄìOct). Cave tours year-round. Some roads close in winter.  
- **3 activities:** Lehman Caves tour; drive to Wheeler Peak + bristlecone pine grove hike; astronomy program or self-guided stargazing (certified Dark Sky Park).  
- **Lodging:** Limited on-site camping; motels/B&Bs in Baker or Ely, NV.  
- **Hook:** "brunch ‚Üí cave wonder ‚Üí ancient trees ‚Üí darkest skies"  
- **Insider angle:** Lean into "interned there" / ranger community‚Äîreal connection and solitude.
