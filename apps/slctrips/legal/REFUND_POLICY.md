# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## 1. Overview

At SLCTrips, we strive to provide high-quality digital content and exceptional customer service. This Refund Policy explains our approach to refunds for TripKit purchases and other digital products.

**Important:** Digital content purchases are generally non-refundable once downloaded or accessed. Please review TripKit descriptions and previews carefully before purchasing.

## 2. General Refund Policy

### 2.1 Digital Content Nature
- TripKits are digital products delivered electronically
- Upon purchase, you receive immediate access to digital content
- Once content is accessed or downloaded, it cannot be "returned"
- All sales are generally considered final

### 2.2 No-Questions-Asked Refund Period
We offer a **7-day satisfaction guarantee** for all TripKit purchases:

**Conditions:**
- Request must be made within 7 days of purchase
- No more than 3 refund requests per account per year
- Account must be in good standing (no violations of Terms of Service)

**How to Request:**
1. Contact support at Dan@slctrips.com within 7 days
2. Provide order number and brief reason for refund
3. Refunds processed within 5-10 business days

### 2.3 Refund Method
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Processing time: 5-10 business days via Stripe
- Payment processor fees may not be refundable
- Refunds appear as credits on your statement (timing varies by bank)

## 3. Refund Eligibility

### 3.1 Eligible for Refund

**Full Refund (within 7 days):**
- TripKit does not match its description
- Technical issues prevent access to purchased content
- Duplicate purchases (automatic full refund)
- Unauthorized charges (with verification)
- Change of mind (within 7-day window)

**Partial Refund (case-by-case):**
- Missing content or features as described
- Significant errors in content
- Service disruption preventing content access

**Account Credit (at our discretion):**
- Multiple issues with purchased content
- Long-term access problems
- Customer loyalty considerations

### 3.2 NOT Eligible for Refund

Refunds will generally be denied for:

- Requests made more than 7 days after purchase (unless exceptional circumstances)
- Change of plans or inability to travel
- Content that meets its description but doesn't suit personal preferences
- Accounts with history of refund abuse
- Content accessed or downloaded from accounts with Terms of Service violations
- Promotional or discounted purchases (unless legally required)
- Bundle purchases where individual items were accessed
- Free content (TK-000 Guardian content)

## 4. Specific Product Policies

### 4.1 TripKit Purchases

**Individual TripKits:**
- 7-day refund period from purchase date
- Full refund if content not accessed
- Discretionary refund if accessed within 7 days

**TripKit Bundles:**
- 7-day refund period applies to entire bundle
- If any kit in bundle is accessed, refund may be partial
- Refund value calculated based on accessed vs. unaccessed content

**Pre-Order TripKits (if applicable):**
- Full refund available anytime before content release
- Standard 7-day policy applies after release

### 4.2 TK-000 Guardian Content

**Free Educational Content:**
- TK-000 county profiles are free
- No refunds applicable (no purchase made)
- Access may be revoked for Terms of Service violations

### 4.3 Future Subscription Services (if implemented)

**Monthly/Annual Subscriptions:**
- Refunds prorated for unused portion
- Cancellation effective at end of current billing period
- No refunds for partial month usage

## 5. Technical Issues and Service Disruptions

### 5.1 Platform Issues
If technical problems prevent access to purchased content:

**Our Responsibility:**
- Server outages or platform downtime
- Broken download links
- Corrupted files or missing content
- Authentication or access errors

**Resolution:**
1. Contact support immediately
2. We will attempt to resolve technical issues within 48 hours
3. If unresolved, full refund or account credit offered
4. Extended access may be provided as compensation

### 5.2 User-Side Issues
We generally cannot offer refunds for:
- Poor internet connection
- Device incompatibility (check system requirements)
- User error or misunderstanding of features
- Lost account credentials (we can help recover)

**Exception:** If technical requirements were not clearly stated, we may offer partial refund or credit.

## 6. Refund Process

### 6.1 How to Request a Refund

**Step 1: Contact Support**
- Email: Dan@slctrips.com
- Subject: "Refund Request - Order #[your order number]"
- Include:
  - Order number or purchase confirmation
  - Purchase date
  - TripKit name(s)
  - Reason for refund request
  - Account email

**Step 2: Review**
- Our team will review your request within 2 business days
- We may ask clarifying questions
- We may attempt to resolve issues before processing refund

**Step 3: Decision**
- You'll receive an email with our decision
- If approved: Refund processed to original payment method
- If denied: Explanation provided with any alternative solutions

**Step 4: Processing**
- Approved refunds processed within 5-10 business days
- Stripe handles the actual refund transaction
- You'll receive confirmation email when refund is issued

### 6.2 Timeline
- **Request Review:** 1-2 business days
- **Refund Processing:** 5-10 business days
- **Bank Credit:** Varies by financial institution (typically 3-5 additional days)

**Total Time:** 10-20 business days from request to bank credit

## 7. Disputed Charges and Chargebacks

### 7.1 Contact Us First
**Before initiating a chargeback with your bank:**
- Contact our support team
- Most issues can be resolved quickly
- Chargebacks can take 60-90 days and may result in account suspension

### 7.2 Chargeback Consequences
If you initiate a chargeback without contacting us:
- Your account may be suspended during investigation
- Access to purchased content may be revoked
- We will provide transaction evidence to payment processor
- If chargeback is found fraudulent, account will be permanently banned

### 7.3 Fraudulent Charges
If you believe a charge is fraudulent:
- Contact us immediately
- We will investigate and refund if unauthorized
- Change your password and enable two-factor authentication
- We may file fraud reports for account protection

## 8. Promotional and Discounted Purchases

### 8.1 Sale Purchases
- Standard 7-day refund policy applies
- Refund amount is the discounted price you paid (not full price)
- Sale items cannot be returned after sale period for price adjustment

### 8.2 Coupon or Promo Code Purchases
- Refund is the amount actually charged
- Promo codes may not be reissued for refunded purchases
- One-time promo codes are not transferable to new purchases

### 8.3 Bundle Deals
- If bundle accessed partially, refund calculated on unaccessed content
- Bundle pricing may mean individual items have no separate refund value

## 9. Account Closure and Content Access

### 9.1 Refunds and Account Closure
- Closing your account does NOT entitle you to refunds for past purchases
- Purchased content older than 7 days is non-refundable
- Recent purchases within 7-day window may be refunded

### 9.2 Content Access After Refund
- Upon refund approval, access to refunded content is immediately revoked
- Downloaded files should be deleted
- Continued use of refunded content may violate copyright law

### 9.3 Partial Refunds and Access
- For partial refunds, access to paid portions continues
- Refunded portions are removed from your account

## 10. Exceptions and Special Circumstances

### 10.1 Discretionary Refunds
We reserve the right to issue refunds outside this policy for:
- Exceptional customer service situations
- Repeated technical issues
- Billing errors on our part
- Legal requirements
- Medical or family emergencies (with documentation)

### 10.2 Store Credit Alternative
In some cases, we may offer store credit instead of refund:
- Requests outside 7-day window
- Partial bundle refunds
- Technical issue compensation
- Customer loyalty rewards

**Store Credit Terms:**
- Never expires
- Can be used for any TripKit purchase
- Not transferable or redeemable for cash
- Remains in account even after closure (can be used if account reopened)

### 10.3 Bulk or Educational Purchases
Special refund terms may apply for:
- School or educational institution purchases
- Group or organizational licenses (if implemented)
- Enterprise or commercial use agreements

Contact our sales team for custom refund terms.
