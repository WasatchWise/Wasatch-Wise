## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Account & Login

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page. You'll receive a reset link via email within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Yes! Log in to your account, go to Settings, and update your email. You'll need to verify the new email address.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact support with your request. Note that account deletion is permanent and purchased content will no longer be accessible (subject to our refund policy for recent purchases).

**Q: I'm not receiving emails from SLCTrips. What should I do?**
A: Check your spam/junk folder and add our email addresses to your contacts. If issues persist, contact support - we can verify your email settings.

### TripKit Purchases

**Q: How do I access my purchased TripKits?**
A: Log in to your account and visit "My TripKits" in your dashboard. All purchased content is available for download and viewing there.

**Q: Can I download TripKits for offline use?**
A: Yes! TripKits are designed to be downloaded as PDFs for offline access during your travels.

**Q: Can I share my TripKit with family/friends?**
A: TripKits are licensed for personal use only. Sharing or distributing purchased content violates our Terms of Service. Consider purchasing additional licenses for travel companions.

**Q: Do TripKits expire?**
A: No! Once purchased, you have lifetime access to your TripKits as long as your account remains active.

**Q: What's the difference between free TK-000 content and paid TripKits?**
A: TK-000 features our Guardian County Profiles - free educational content about Utah's 29 counties. Paid TripKits offer premium curated itineraries, deep-dive stories, insider tips, and specialized travel planning resources.

### Billing & Refunds

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) processed securely through Stripe.

**Q: Is my payment information secure?**
A: Absolutely! We use Stripe for payment processing - they're PCI DSS compliant and handle millions of secure transactions daily. We never store your complete card information.

**Q: How do I request a refund?**
A: Email Dan@slctrips.com within 7 days of purchase with your order number. See our [Refund Policy](./REFUND_POLICY.md) for full details.

**Q: I was charged twice for the same purchase. What do I do?**
A: Contact Dan@slctrips.com immediately with your order details. Duplicate charges are refunded automatically and processed within 5-10 business days.

**Q: Do you offer discounts or promo codes?**
A: Yes! Sign up for our newsletter to receive exclusive discounts, seasonal promotions, and early access to new TripKits.

### Technical Issues

**Q: TripKit download link isn't working. Help!**
A: Try these steps:
1. Refresh the page and try again
2. Try a different browser
3. Check your internet connection
4. Clear browser cache and cookies

If still not working, email Dan@slctrips.com with your order number.

**Q: Images aren't loading properly. What's wrong?**
A: This is usually a connection issue. Try:
1. Refreshing the page
2. Checking your internet speed
3. Disabling browser extensions/ad blockers
4. Trying a different device

If issues persist, contact technical support.

**Q: The website is running slowly. Is there an issue?**
A: Occasional slowdowns can happen due to high traffic. Try:
1. Clearing browser cache
2. Using a different browser
3. Checking our status page

For persistent issues, let us know at Dan@slctrips.com.

**Q: I found a broken link or error on the site. How do I report it?**
A: Thank you for helping us improve! Email Dan@slctrips.com with:
- The page URL where you found the issue
- Description of the problem
- Screenshot (if possible)
- Your browser and device type

### Guardian Content

**Q: What are the Guardian County Profiles (TK-000)?**
A: Our Guardians are 29 unique characters representing each Utah county! They narrate educational stories about their county's history, geography, and culture in a fun, engaging way appropriate for 4th-grade level learners. This content is FREE for everyone!

**Q: Can I use Guardian content in my classroom?**
A: Yes! TK-000 Guardian content is free for educational use. For large-scale distribution or curriculum integration, please contact Dan@slctrips.com.

**Q: Who created the Guardian characters?**
A: Each Guardian was carefully crafted by the SLCTrips team based on the unique characteristics, history, and personality of their county. From Bruno the coal-loving guardian of Carbon County to the vibrant spirits of Salt Lake, each has their own story!

**Q: Will there be more Guardian content?**
A: We're constantly expanding! Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when new Guardian stories are released.

### Travel Planning

**Q: Are the destinations and information up-to-date?**
A: We strive to keep all information current, but destinations, hours, and conditions can change. Always verify details before traveling, especially:
- Operating hours
- Seasonal closures
- Road conditions
- Permit requirements

**Q: Do you offer custom trip planning services?**
A: Currently, our TripKits provide curated itineraries and planning resources. For custom trip planning inquiries, contact Dan@slctrips.com.

**Q: Can I suggest a destination to be added?**
A: Absolutely! We love hearing about hidden gems. Email Dan@slctrips.com with:
- Destination name and location
- Why you think it should be featured
- Any photos or details you'd like to share

**Q: Is SLCTrips affiliated with tourism boards or destinations?**
A: We're an independent platform. While we may partner with tourism organizations, all content is created objectively to provide authentic travel planning resources.
