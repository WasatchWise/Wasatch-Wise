# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Questions

### What is SLCTrips?

SLCTrips is a comprehensive Utah adventure platform featuring over 1,000 destinations, curated TripKits (digital guidebooks), and 29 county-based Guardian characters. We help you discover amazing places from 30 minutes to 12 hours from Salt Lake City International Airport.

### What is a TripKit?

A TripKit is a digital guidebook containing curated destinations, routes, insider tips, and detailed information about a specific theme or region. Think of it as your complete adventure packageâ€”everything you need to explore Utah's best experiences.

### What is a Guardian?

Guardians are 29 mythical characters, each representing one of Utah's counties. They serve as your guides to hidden gems and local insights across the state. Each Guardian has their own personality, backstory, and recommendations.

### Is SLCTrips free?

We offer both free and paid content:

- **Free:** TK-000 "Free Utah" educational content with 29 destinations
- **Paid TripKits:** $47-$147+ for curated collections with detailed guides

## Purchases & Access

### How do I purchase a TripKit?

1. Browse our [TripKits page](/tripkits)
2. Click on a TripKit that interests you
3. Click "Purchase TripKit" or "Buy Now"
4. Complete checkout through our secure Stripe payment system
5. You'll receive an access code via email

### How do I access my purchased TripKit?

After purchase, you'll receive an email with:
- Your unique access code
- Instructions on how to use it
- A link to access your TripKit

Simply enter your access code on the TripKit page to unlock all content.

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards and debit cards through Stripe's secure payment processing.

### Can I get a refund?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us at Dan@slctrips.com within 30 days for a full refund. See our [Refund Policy](/legal/refund) for complete details.

### I didn't receive my access code. What should I do?

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify the email address you used for purchase
3. Contact us at Dan@slctrips.com with your order details
4. We'll resend your access code within 24 hours

### Can I share my access code with others?

Access codes are intended for personal use. Sharing codes violates our Terms of Service. Each purchase grants access to one user.

## Content & Destinations

### How many destinations do you have?

We currently feature over 1,000 destinations across Utah, with more being added regularly.

### How do you choose destinations?

Destinations are curated based on:
- Local knowledge and insider recommendations
- Accessibility from Salt Lake City International Airport
- Unique experiences and hidden gems
- Seasonal considerations
- Value and quality

### Can I suggest a destination?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our community. Email us at Dan@slctrips.com with:
- Destination name and location
- Why you think it's special
- Any photos or details you'd like to share

### Are destinations updated regularly?

Yes! We continuously update destination information, add new places, and refresh content. Stale or outdated destinations are regularly reviewed and updated.

### What's the difference between a destination and a TripKit?

- **Destination:** A single place or experience (e.g., "Antelope Island State Park")
- **TripKit:** A curated collection of destinations organized by theme (e.g., "Ski Utah Complete" with 50+ ski destinations)
