## Priority Concepts

### 1. Continental Divide Trail (CDT) - Utah Segment
**Status:** Concept - Flagged for Development
**Potential Replacement For:** Lower-scoring hiking/backpacking TripKits
**Target Audience:**
- Expert backpackers
- CDT thru-hikers
- High Uintas adventurers
- Triple Crown hikers

**Value Proposition:**
- **Unique Intel:** Life-saving safety information for one of America's most remote trails
- **Niche Market:** CDT hikers are highly engaged, travel-focused audience
- **High Price Point:** Expert-level content justifies premium pricing
- **Low Competition:** Very few comprehensive guides for Utah CDT segment
- **Affiliate Potential:** High-value gear recommendations (PLB, bear canisters, GPS)

**Content Outline:**
1. **Trail Overview**
   - 160-mile Utah segment details
   - Elevation profile and difficulty rating
   - Season-by-season conditions
   - Permit requirements

2. **Critical Safety Intel**
   - Zero cell service protocol
   - Emergency contact procedures
   - Wildlife encounter protocols (bears, moose, lions)
   - Weather hazard mitigation
   - Lightning safety above treeline

3. **Resupply Strategy**
   - Manila, UT logistics
   - Mirror Lake Highway access (seasonal)
   - Evanston, WY options
   - Food/gear recommendations
   - Water source reliability by month

4. **Navigation & Logistics**
   - GPS waypoints
   - Alternate routes
   - Bail-out points
   - Nearest medical facilities
   - Trailhead access and parking

5. **Gear Requirements**
   - Bear canister specifications
   - PLB/satellite messenger comparison
   - Navigation tools (GPS + paper maps)
   - Cold-weather gear (even in summer)
   - Water filtration strategy

6. **Section Highlights**
   - High Uintas Wilderness traverse
   - Kings Peak proximity (Utah's highest)
   - Alpine lake camping
   - Photography opportunities

**Monetization:**
- Premium pricing ($29-49)
- Affiliate gear (PLB, GPS, bear canister = high $ items)
- Potential partnership with CDT Association
- Downloadable GPX files
- Printable maps and checklists

**Data Requirements:**
- Trail GPS waypoints
- Water source database (seasonal)
- Resupply town details
- Emergency contact list
- Weather station data
- Permit office contacts

**Guardian Integration:**
- **Ira (Daggett County)** - Osprey, Manila area, CDT northern entry
- **Vex (Summit County)** - Fox, Mirror Lake Highway, High Uintas access
- **Elsa (Cache County)** - Honeybee
- **Rich (Rich County)** - Lake Serpent
- Ancient trails, mountain wisdom, survival lore

## TripKit Performance Review Process

### Monthly Review
1. **Identify Bottom 10% Performers**
   - Sales volume
   - Download/completion rate
   - User ratings
   - Affiliate click-through
   - Time spent in kit

2. **Root Cause Analysis**
   - Poor content quality?
   - Weak target audience fit?
   - Outdated information?
   - Better alternatives available?
   - SEO/discoverability issues?

3. **Decision Matrix**
   - **Improve:** Update content, add features, re-market
   - **Replace:** Develop new kit for same category
   - **Retire:** Remove and redirect to better alternative
   - **Bundle:** Combine with higher-performing kit

### Replacement Priority
Replace TripKits with:
1. Low engagement + low sales
2. Outdated seasonal content
3. Weak unique value proposition
4. High refund rates
5. Negative user feedback
